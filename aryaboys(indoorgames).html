INDOOR GAMES

Children nowadays don't even know about the joy of playing outdoor games. And Psychiatrists say that children who do not get play in the open may have alarming consequences on their health and overall development. In fact, experts even say that the lack of outdoor and indoor games in the child's daily timetable may have serious emotional repercussions and might even impair the social skills among children.
